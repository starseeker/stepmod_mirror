6 STEP Module and Resource Library

6.1 Series of parts

The STEP Module and Resource Library (SMRL) includes the following documents:

  • application modules series of parts: ISO 10303 parts 401 to 499 and 1001 to 1999;
  • application interpreted constructs series of parts: ISO 10303 parts 501 to 599;
  • integrated resources series of parts: ISO 10303 parts 41 to 59;
  • logical model of expressions: ISO 13584-20.

6.2 Navigation

Each part belonging to the SMRL is maintained as a separate ISO document. However, the individual parts belonging to the SMRL will not be sold or distributed separately; they will only be sold and distributed as part of a package consisting of the entire SMRL. The SMRL product contains a top-level structure that provides navigation indices for the parts that are included in the SMRL.

The modular APs make use of the application modules in the SMRL.

Other APs make use of the AICs and IRs.
