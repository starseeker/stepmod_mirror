AP 203 implementation information

ISO 10303-203 (AP203) development is sponsored by PDES, Inc. (http://pdesinc.aticorp.org)

Recommended practices or usage guidelines for many capabilities of AP203 are available from:

  • PDM Implementor's Forum (http://www.pdm-if.org/) for product data management, in the PDM Schema Usage Guide.
  • CAx Implementor's Forum (http://www.cax-if.org/) for mechanical design, under Joint Testing Information.

An experimental wiki for recommended practices is under construction at http://www.wikistep.org/.
