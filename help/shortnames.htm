Registering shortnames

You can generate and register the shortnames on the STEPtools website. http://www.steptools.com/sc4/services/index.html

You will need to login as an anonymous user.

You should upload your mim.exp file and generate the shortnames.

Once they have been generated, copy them for the modules.xml file and then hit the "Submit report" button on the "Output not wanted pane(as below).

   PRODUCT_DESIGN_TO_INDIVIDUAL                            PDTI   (new)
   PRODUCT_PLANNED_TO_REALIZED                             PPTR   (new)

Adding shortnames to your module

Add the shortnames as part of the mim definition in module.xml.

E.g. 

   <mim>
     <express-g>
     <imgfile file="mimexpg1.xml"/>
     <imgfile file="mimexpg2.xml"/>
    </express-g>
    <shortnames>
      <shortname name="PRASIN" entity="product_as_individual"/>
      <shortname name="PRASPL" entity="product_as_planned"/>
      <shortname name="PRASRL" entity="product_as_realized"/>
      <shortname name="PDTI" entity="product_design_to_individual"/>
      <shortname name="PPTR" entity="product_planned_to_realized"/>
    </shortnames>
  </mim>
